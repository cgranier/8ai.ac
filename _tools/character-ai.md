Character.ai is a platform where users can create and interact with AI-powered characters. Founded by former Google Brain researchers, it became one of the most popular consumer AI products with millions of daily users. Users can chat with pre-built characters or create their own with custom personalities, making it popular for entertainment, creative writing, roleplay, and language practice.
